Teachings of Jesus

He is the light of the world.

These teachings of Jesus Christ are principles I try to live by. They are connected to the scriptures and ideas shared throughout this page and have helped shape my faith and perspective on life.

Love Others

Jesus Christ taught us to love others as He loves us. This means showing kindness, patience, and compassion in all situations.

“Love one another; as I have loved you.” (John 13:34)

Faith in Hard Times

Faith in Jesus Christ gives us strength during difficult moments. It reminds us that we are never alone and that God has a plan for us, even when life feels uncertain.

“Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you… Let not your heart be troubled.” (John 14:27)

“The Lord knows what you’re going through… He loves you.” — Russell M. Nelson

Service

Serving others is one of the greatest ways to follow Jesus Christ. When we help others, we become more like Him and bring more light into the world.

“When ye are in the service of your fellow beings…” (Mosiah 2:17)

Forgiveness

Forgiveness brings peace and healing. Jesus Christ showed us that we should forgive others, even when it is difficult, so we can move forward with love.

“Forgive, and ye shall be forgiven.” (Luke 6:37)

The Book of Mormon

The Book of Mormon is another testament of Jesus Christ and is a powerful source of truth and guidance. It helps strengthen my faith and gives clear direction on how to live a Christ-centered life.

Through its teachings, I have learned more about the Savior, felt peace in difficult times, and found answers to important questions.

“Feast upon the words of Christ; for behold, the words of Christ will tell you all things what ye should do.” (2 Nephi 32:3)
